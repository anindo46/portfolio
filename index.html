About Me

I am currently an undergraduate student specializing in Geology and Mining at the University of Barishal. In addition to my academic pursuits, I serve as a Research Fellow at the Barishal University Research Society (BURS). My work is centered on advancing the understanding of environmental systems through rigorous geospatial analysis and data-driven methodologies.

My research interests encompass Land Use and Land Cover (LULC) change detection, NDVI and NDWI analyses, air quality assessment, and climate resilience modeling. I am proficient in utilizing advanced geospatial tools, including ArcGIS, QGIS, ArcGIS Pro, Google Earth Engine, and Python, to address complex environmental challenges. Through my efforts, I strive to integrate scientific research with practical solutions aimed at fostering sustainable development and environmental resilience.

Field Work Experience

Sitakund
2023
▸

2023 – Sitakund: Participated in geological mapping in the Sitakund anticline region. The focus was on stratigraphic exposure, fault interpretation, and slope stability analysis. I documented lithological sequences, measured dip and strike using compass clinometer, and created field logs with detailed observations. The work contributed to a structural profile of the fold belt, and we presented our results through annotated cross-sections. This hands-on exposure helped me understand real-world sedimentary and tectonic processes.

Cox's Bazar
Feb 2025
▸

During the February 2025 field session in Cox’s Bazar, we investigated coastal geomorphology, active sedimentary processes, and stratigraphic exposures along the marine cliffs and beach systems. Our work included shoreline profiling, dune–beach morphology observations, and documentation of sedimentary structures related to wave and tidal action. We also examined lithologic variations in cliff exposures to understand depositional settings and possible neotectonic influences. This fieldwork strengthened my understanding of coastal dynamics, erosion patterns, sediment transport pathways, and hazard-prone coastal environments.
