About Me

I am an undergraduate researcher in Geology and Mining at the University of Barishal, actively working on geospatial analysis, environmental modeling, and climate-focused research. My work centers on shoreline change analysis, land use and land cover (LULC) dynamics, and remote sensing-based environmental monitoring. I use tools such as Google Earth Engine, ArcGIS, QGIS, and Python to process and analyze large-scale spatial datasets, applying both statistical and machine learning approaches where necessary. Through my research, I aim to understand how coastal and environmental systems evolve over time and to generate insights that can support sustainable planning, climate adaptation, and evidence-based decision-making.
