Building Insights from Earth Data

Geospatial Analysis · Remote Sensing · Climate Data Modeling

I analyze shoreline dynamics, vegetation change, and environmental trends using remote sensing, GIS, and time-series data. My work focuses on uncovering spatial patterns and understanding how environmental systems evolve over time, particularly in climate-sensitive coastal regions.

As an undergraduate researcher in Geology and Mining at the University of Barishal, I work with tools such as Google Earth Engine, ArcGIS, QGIS, and Python to process and interpret large-scale spatial datasets. My research includes NDVI time-series analysis, LULC change detection, and environmental monitoring, aiming to support sustainable planning and climate resilience.
